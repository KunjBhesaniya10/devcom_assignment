TRAVELLING & ADVENTURE




I love to visit new places and have exciting adventures. Traveling helps me learn about different cultures, languages, and lifestyles. It also challenges me to try new things and overcome my fears. Till today, I have visited many places like Kutch, Udaipur, Chittorgarh, Delhi, Kurukshetra, Girnar mountain, Gir forest ( home of Asiatic lions and also my hometown), and of course, Mumbai. I have an excellent time roaming with friends in various places in Mumbai, like Marine Drive, Juhu Beach, Hiranandani, magical malls, and movie theatres. I am looking forward to visiting many other places here. Traveling is my hobby, and I want to see more places worldwide. My dream is to travel to different places around the world.
