TABLE TENNIS


Table tennis has been my passion since 6th grade. By 8th grade, I had achieved a commendable skill level. Representing my school, I competed at the regional level in Under-14 for Inter-NVS 2018 and national at Inter-NVS 2019 Under-14 competition from the Jaipur region, adding a significant milestone to my journey With Table tennis. However, JEE preparation led me to set aside table tennis for 2-3 years momentarily. After completing JEE, I eagerly returned to the game, determined to regain and surpass my previous level. With an aggressive playing style, I find joy in the precision and strategy of table tennis, making it an integral part of my identity.
